About Kumusha Events & Entertainment

Connecting exceptional talent with unforgettable events since 2025

Our Story

At Kumusha Events & Entertainment, our mission is to make it easy for event planners, companies, and individuals to find exceptional entertainment acts in Cape Town and beyond.

What started as a small directory of local performers has grown into a comprehensive platform connecting clients with hundreds of talented acts across multiple categories.

We pride ourselves on our curated selection process, ensuring that every act in our network meets our high standards for professionalism, talent, and reliability.
